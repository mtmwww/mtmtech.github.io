Company: CNC Manufacturing

Machine Motor Vibration Monitoring


Problem:


Unexpected machine downtime halts the manufacturing line resulting in great monetary losses. The factory staff can only do periodic maintenance for these CNC machines based on their own judgement and personal experience.



Solution:


DCS with Vibration Detector is installed to monitor the vibration patterns of each CNC machine. Often before a machine breaks down, irregular vibration patterns occurs. When abnormal vibration patterns are detected, a warning message can be sent and proper maintenance can be scheduled before a machine breakdown.
