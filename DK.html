MtM IoT Development Kit

Our IoT development kit contains a range of connectivity & processing boards, sensor boards, and accessory boards. Modular and stackable boards means you can pick the functionality you need, stack them together, and develop your product. Our kit comes with ready-to-use firmware for a range of sensors, so once you stack the boards together and load the firmware, you can immediately see sensor data on the phone. (and soon the cloud!) Our boards themsevles are small enough for some, and may function as a complete DIY device by just adding a casing around the stacked boards. If you are serious about productizing your project though, our modules are mass-production-ready, and are in use in various products in the market. Our development kit is also the only kit in the market to offer SiP (System-in-Package) modules in the kit, which means functionalities are packed into a very small form factor. This makes it possible to develop very small or portable products.

MtConnect03

BLE + MCU + Motion


  Usage


MtConnect03 is a multi-function board which contains our M904 BLE + MCU module, and also motion sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope). The M904 is a tiny 6.5 x 6.5mm module hosting a Cortex M0 MCU, and has the Antenna embedded in the module package. Together with motion sensors, this board alone is enough for creating some very interesting applications.

Accessories

MtAid01

Extension


Use this extention board to connect to other peripherals or third-party boards that you would like to add to your project. Because our boards are small, this extension board also serves as the bridge for programming your device. But once your device is loaded with the desired code, the small boards can function on their own.

MtAid02

Coin Battery


Once you programmed your MtConnect board the way you like, the stacked boards can function as a standalone device with just this coin battery board. Because BLE is low power, you can do a lot with just a coin battery. (Like run a beacon device for a year. Or a pedometer device for days.))

MtAid03

USB Interface


Use this board for powering your device from USB, or for programming other USB-interface related features.

MtAid04

RGB LED


It's always more fun when your project is more "visual". Use this RGB LED as an indicator device, or as a smart lighting LED device. RGBW LED means you can have millions of colors to play with. Flash green when there's an incoming call? Or flash pink when your loved ones arrive home?

MtAid05

Arduino Extension


This board allows you to do everything you can do using MtAid01, but in addition, it let's you do Arduino programming on our MtConnect04S board. If you come from the Arduino world, or if you want to tap into the Arduino resources to kick start your project, then this board is for you!
