Company: Petrochemical Industry

Power Meter Image Recognition


Problem:


Each day, one operator is required to manually record all the power meter readings spanned over 60 locations. The daily electricity power consumption at each factory is also rationed and is fined if there are over-usage.



Solution:


DCS with IP camera is installed to monitor the reading of the power meters. This provided real time monitoring and recording of the power readings and can also send warning messages if neccessary.
