Smart Factory Solution

Real-time assets and environment monitoring

Our solution has two main features: asset management and environment monitoring. With real-time detecting and automatically alert, we help you to manage your factory in a more safety, efficiency, and cost savings way.

Asset Management

From tracking devices in factories to charting workers in exposed environments, our asset management offers massive opportunities to almost every sector. Based on real-time location system (RTLS), the manager can know where assets are located, processes can be streamlined, generating valuable data and providing increased safety, efficiency, and cost savings solution.

Check in / Check out

Record each time an asset enters or exits a space.

Taking Stock

Monitor weather assets are located in a given area or not.

Analyze & Alert

Record movements for analysis and show alert when the asset left the space.

Environmental Monitoring

Based on industry 4.0, our intelligent control system used wireless sensors to monitor the quality of the environment. Once the environment changes or not in normal range, the sensor will show the alert.

Wireless Sensor

Use Bluetooth low energy (BLE) to transmit data.

Environment Control

Measure temperature, humidity and atmospheric pressure.

Real-time Alerts

Alart immediately when environmet reached the setting point.

Where can be used in practical?

Nitrogen cabinet

Detect temperature and humidity inside the cabinet to make sure it is costantly.
