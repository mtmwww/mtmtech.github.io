Smart Factory Solution

Real-time assets and environment monitoring

Our solution has two main features: asset menagement and environment monitoring. With real-time detecting and automatically alert, we help you to manage your factory in a more safety, efficiency, and cost savings way.

Asset Management

From tracking devices in factories to charting workers in exposed environments, our asset management offers massive opportunities to almost every sector. Based on real-time location system (RTLS), the manager can know where assets are located, processes can be streamlined, generating valuable data and providing increased safety, efficiency, and cost savings solution.

Check in/Check out

Record each time an asset enters or exits a space.

Taking Stock

Monitor weather assets are located in a given area or not.

Analyze and Alert

Record movements for analysis and show alert when the asset left the space.

Application

Office managers :know employee movements to verify attendance.
Equipment : track machine's location.
Warehouse : diminish transport error, optimize storage and storing processes.

Environmental Monitoring

Based on industry 4.0, our intelligent control system used wireless sensors to monitor the quality of the environment. Once the environmet changes or not in normal range, sensor will show alert.

Wireless Sensor

Use Bluetooth low energy (BLE) to transmit data.

Environment Control

Measuring temperature, humidity and atmospheric pressure.

Real-time Alerts

When environmet changed (or reached the setting point ) manager will get the alart immediately.

Application

Wafer cabinet: detect temperature and humidity inside the cabinet to make sure it is costantly.
Facility: check environment status is good for facilities.
Laboratory: detect steam is or not suitable for experiment.
