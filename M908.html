Design

We are here for you

Intel Quark SE C1000

Low-power-consumption system-on-chip that provides edge analytics by combining an x86 MCU with a sensor subsystem and pattern-matching capability through a hardware-accelerated engine.

Semtech SX1276

The Semtech SX1276 carries LoRa® long range modem that provides ultra-long range spread spectrum communication and high interference immunity whilst minimising current consumption.

SiP Technology

As the IoT becomes more of a reality and less of a vision, there is innovation going on at the system on a chip and SiP level so that microcontroller and RF transceiver can be integrated on a separate die and control the connectivity.
