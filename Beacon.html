Applications

Proximity Sensing

Beacon devices continually broadcast signals with specific information identitying itself. Using this information the system can determine which beacon (or location) a user is in proximity with, and provide location-specific information to the user. Retail stores can use this technology to send product information or promotional information to customers approaching differnt products. Museums can also use this technology to provide information of different items under exhibition as visitors approach different items.

Indoor Navigation

Beacon technology can be combined with additional algorithms to provide indoor navigation services. With beacons carefully placed within an indoor area, the locatiton of different users can be pin-pointed on the indoor map. The service can then guide the user to the destination. This will be very useful for large indoor shopping malls, or exhibitions or conventions

Find Me / Theft Prevention

Beacon technology can also be used for "find me" or "theft prevention" tag devices. Attach the tag to things you fear losing, and when the tag becomes far away from your phone, the APP on your phone will alert you. Attach the tag to your valuables, and if someone is taking it away from you, your phone alerts you. Check the last-know GPS location if you happen to lose your object.
