Silicon Wafer Smart Storage Monitoring



Problem:


Silicon wafers are stored in nitrogen cabinets to prevent oxidation. The temperature, humidity and nitrogen level inside the cabinet changes due to environmental factors as well as the opening and closing of the cabinets. The current solution to ensure the proper nitrogen level is to slowly release N2 constantly.



Solution:


DCS with Environmental Sensor is installed to monitor the temperature and humidty inside the nitrogen storage cabinets. This provided real time monitoring of these two matrices and nitrogen is replenished accordingly.

Improvements:


Since nitrogen is released only when the temperature and humidity exceeded a certain level, this has resulted in nitrogen savings up to 40%! Valueable historical data is also collected to see when nitrogen is released the most and more strategic storage of wafers is applied to further the savings.
