BLE SiP Module

M905

The MtM+ M905 is the smallest Bluetooth low energy (BLE) SiP module with a built-in antenna. It is an ARM® Cortex™-M4F 32bit based processor and is designed for Bluetooth® Smart and IoT solutions.

Low power consumption

7.5mA when TX at +4dBm output power.

NFC touch to pair

Also with hardware divider, FPU and DAC

4.2

Bluetooth

Increase speed and reliability of data transfer between BLE devices. Specifically for IoT application.

Built-in Antenna

The antenna is built on SiP package.
No extra space is needed for your design.

Audio

Capable for digital microphone and voice input speech recognition control.

Bluetooth Mesh

The addition of mesh networking capabilities to Bluetooth Low Energy enables extended range, increases the number of nodes and a many-to-many (m:m) communication topology.

Feature List

  • Multi-protocol with Bluetooth low energy / ANT / 2.4G RF
  • Built-in antenna
  • 32-bit ARM® Cortex™-M4F processor
  • 512kB flash + 64kB RAM
  • LGA-52 package, 6.5 ✕ 6.5 x 1.1 mm
  • Application development independent from protocol stack
  • Programmable output power from +4dBm to -20dBm
  • Flexible and configurable 26 pin GPIO
  • Simple ON/OFF global power modes
  • Digital interfaces including: SPI/2-wire/UART/PDM/I²S
  • 12-bit/200KSPS ADC
  • 128-bit AES ECB/CCM/AAR co-processor
  • NFC touch-to-pair
  • Quadrature demodulator
  • Low power 32MHz crystal inside the module
  • Ultra-low power 32kHz crystal and RC oscillators for best power consumption
  • Wide supply voltage range (1.7 V to 3.6 V)
  • On-chip DC/DC buck converter
  • Individual power management for all peripherals
  • FCC, CE and BQB certification (under going)
